About Greetings from JRL

The app Greetings from JRL is a small presentation of it's developer, Jon Robinson Levy. It is developed in C#/.Net using Xamarin (Forms) and MvvmCross for iOS, Android and UWP. It is only publicly available for Android and UWP, however.

The app is a sort of mini-game, where you explore various rooms in order to learn more about the developer. If you find the hidden room, and solve all tasks and puzzles, you may even have saved the world and received a reward.

Note: the app is not supposed to be beautiful: simplicity, ideas, and texts are the focus.

The app was originally intended for a specific potential employer (and I got the job, but then I didn't… long story), this is a modified, general version. The original version didn't include a back end, this one does (just for show), which is hosted on this site.
